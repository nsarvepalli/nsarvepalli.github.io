Food Inspection Analytics Platform

  • SCD2 star schema in ER Studio with standardized dim_ and fact_ tables across both cities
  • 66 column source-to-target mappings documenting every transformation
  • 8-page Power BI dashboard with drill-through into risk categories, facility types, violation codes, and geographic hotspots
